Concepts

Roll

New points on one surface contact new points on the opposing surface. Like a tire rolling on pavement.

Glide (Slide)

The same point on one surface contacts new points on the other. Like a wheel skidding.

Spin

Rotation around a fixed point of contact. Like a spinning top.
